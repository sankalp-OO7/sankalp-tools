Company Overview
Where Artificial Intelligence
Meets the Global Forex Market

Shams Global Systems stands at the intersection of machine intelligence and financial markets — delivering tools once reserved exclusively for institutional desks, now made accessible to individual traders worldwide. Headquartered on the 18th floor of Ontario Tower in Dubai's prestigious Business Bay, the company has built an algorithmic trading ecosystem of rare sophistication, purpose-designed for the speed and unpredictability of modern Forex markets.

The firm's flagship offering — an AI-powered Expert Advisor (EA) — integrates seamlessly with both MetaTrader 4 and MetaTrader 5, executing with sub-millisecond precision. It monitors global currency pairs around the clock, reacts instantly to market signals, and eliminates the single greatest vulnerability in trading: human emotion.
